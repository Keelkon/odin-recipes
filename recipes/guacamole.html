Guacamole Recipe

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1/2 White Onion, chopped
  • 2 Roma Tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2-1 Lime, juiced
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.

    2. Note: Depending on what you're planning to have with the guacamole depends on how much you want to salt. If just eating with chips, first taste the dip with the chips and add seasoning accordingly. Some chips are salted more than others so you may not need much.

  2. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
